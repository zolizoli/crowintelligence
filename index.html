Hungarian Natural Language Processing Meetup

We are the organizers of Hungarian NLP meetup, which was launched in 2010 and is the biggest Hungarian NLP community with 1400 members. We help recruiters and HR specialists reach a significant number of NLP specialists at the same time. Our offer includes:

We send your job ads to 1400 NLP experts via our mailing list once in a month.

We enable you to meet NLP experts in our meetup. As a meetup sponsor you can introduce your company and offer to the audience and can reach experts in person.

We are open to provide self-tailored offers and combined packages. Please contact us for further details.

Early-phase Hungarian start-ups get 20% discount.

About

Orsolya Putz holds a PhD in Cognitive Linguistics and devotes herself to cognitive sciences. She dreams about a world in which cognitive sciences, AI and data science work hand in hand and jointly produce valuable output, which makes our life better. Currently she is an assistant lecturer at Eötvös Loránd University, Budapest, where she brings together the results of the aforementioned fields. She was member of a data science team of a software developer company.

Zoltán Varjú is the Head of Data Science in SCL Data. He has been working as an expert in Natural Language Processing for 15 years. He has already been applied by small companies, large corporations and NGOs as a software developer, computational linguist, and team leader. Holding MA degrees in Philosophy and Linguistics, he believes that if technology is handled carefully and applied in an ethical way, it can make our world a better place.
